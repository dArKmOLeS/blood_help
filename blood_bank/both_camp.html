What is blood donation camp ?

A blood donation camp is an event where people come together to voluntarily donate blood for those in need. The purpose of blood donation camps is to collect blood from healthy individuals and ensure that blood banks have sufficient stocks of blood to provide to patients in case of medical emergencies, surgeries, or other medical procedures that require blood transfusions.
